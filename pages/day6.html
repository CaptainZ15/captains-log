Day 6: The Fuckening



Captains Log Day 6: Today can only be described as a catastrophe so large that nobody was left untriggered by what occurred on this horrible Thursday night. What began as a relatively peaceful afternoon soon spiraled into unparalleled lunacy and I am sure that things will never be the same after today. My recount of the events that transpired on this dreary night in January are gruesome, but it is necessary that what happened on this day is never to be repeated.



While monitoring communications on the main server, we received an alert from our automated threat assessment center about Cullen the Womanizer. As we read through the chat log to assess the threat, we came across disturbing flirtatious messages towards BonBon, who was rather new to the server. Joanna, in attempt to stop yet another victim of his fake friendship and womanizing ways, teleported to the main server’s chat communications and began to nullify his plan to seduce BonBon.



RazerRazz, who was also new to the main server, was very confused by the situation, and Joanna messaged him about Cullen’s lecherous ways. He sided with Joanna immediately. BonBon was somewhat oblivious as to what was happening in the chat at the moment, but realized that Joanna was typing in the chat. She pined for Joanna’s friendship in that moment, and it seemed that they would be good friends, but that was not to be.



Cullen, jealous that BonBon preferred Joanna over himself, expressed his irritation and his desire to harm himself by drinking poisonous liquids. Noticing his melodrama, Joanna swiftly calls him out on his self-destructive behavior. This only serves to activate his defense mechanisms of being even more melodramatic and emo. Some time passes and as other members of the main server are chatting, Cullen chimes in with an insult about women. Joanna notices the remarkable amount of hypocrisy in his statement due to Cullen’s previous interactions with women, and tells him that if he would stop his womanizing ways, he would be able to find true love.



Cullen takes immense offense to that word, and it set him on the path of total meltdown. As they insult each other back and forth, Cullen slowly gets more and more roasted by Joanna’s logical comebacks and arguments. Cullen, unable to see the logic, had shut his brain off to avoid realizing he was wrong. BonBon was too influenced by Cullen at this point to be able to see reason, and tells Joanna that she is an asshole and should stop insulting Cullen. Refusing all logic and rationality, BonBon also fails to accept the reality of Cullen’s womanizing ways and posts private conversations between her and Joanna in an attempt to invalidate Joanna’s argument. This did not work, however, as most people, myself included, saw that Joanna’s messages to BonBon only further supported her argument.



What was a rather tense and frustrating argument turned into an all-out war the minute Jared entered the conversation. Jared, fueled by the urge to troll, would chime in at every opportune moment to further infuriate the feud between Cullen and Joanna. On one side of the battlefield stood Cullen, with BonBon, Rachel, and Morgan. On the other side stood Joanna, backed by me, Wesley, RazerRazz, Noah, Luke, and Anthony. Corey was a neutral party, and Jared was against everyone. Suddenly, Wesley posts a comment that made so much sense that it got everyone to be silent for a few moments. Those that agreed with him, were taken back by its logic. Those that disagreed, however, were so angry that a council was called for.

As Wesley and Joanna were forcibly kicked off the main server, Noah and Luke fled with them on our escape vessel to the Sanctuary planet that we had discovered. Our plans to return to the main server in a week were a delusion at best, and the permanent residence we have taken up on the Sanctuary planet is enjoyable.



The future is unclear. We are unsure whether or not it will ever be safe to return to the main server, but I feel this will not be the end of our adventures, nor will it be the last entry in the Captains Log. For now, however, this adventure comes to an end, and I sincerely hope that our next one will not be as chaotic.
